The Problem

Why Communities Need Better Flood Warning Systems

Traditional flood monitoring methods in low-lying municipalities often fail to provide sufficient lead time for evacuation.

Insufficient Lead Time

Traditional monitoring methods fail to provide sufficient lead time for evacuation, leaving communities vulnerable during critical moments.

Reliance on Word of Mouth

Families rely on simple water markers or word of mouth, leading to delayed responses and preventable casualties during flood events.

Nighttime & Sudden Surges

Sudden water surges, especially during the rainy season or nighttime flooding, pose a persistent threat with minimal warning windows.

The Solution

Advanced IoT Technology for Flood Detection

A comprehensive system combining precision sensors, dual-microcontroller architecture, and instant alerts.

Precision IoT Detection

Uses three XKC-Y26-V non-contact capacitive liquid level sensors to detect water levels accurately without debris interference.

Dual-Microcontroller Architecture

An Arduino Uno serves as the primary processing unit for local alerts, while an ESP32 acts as the communication gateway for wireless transmission.

Offline Contingency Alerts

Equipped with a physical LED alert tower and a high-decibel piezo buzzer to provide immediate, life-saving warnings even during internet or power interruptions.

Level 1: White
Level 2: Blue
Level 3: Red

Progressive Web Application

Syncs data via a Firebase Realtime Database to a mobile-friendly PWA for remote monitoring and mobile notifications.

Sub-Second Latency

Consistently achieves sub-second data transmission latency and 100% sensor accuracy across all three critical flood stages.

Expert Verified

The structural integrity and technical validity were rigorously tested and verified by DRRM professionals and a licensed architect.
