The Urgent Problem

Why Traditional Warning Systems Fail

Critical gaps in flood monitoring leave vulnerable communities with insufficient lead time during sudden water surges.

Critical Lead Time Gap

Traditional monitoring fails to provide the necessary window for safe evacuation, often leaving residents with only minutes to react.

Communication Failure

Reliance on word-of-mouth and manual markers leads to inconsistent warnings, particularly for isolated or high-risk households.

Sudden Nighttime Surges

Unpredictable surges during heavy rainfall or nighttime floods pose a persistent threat with minimal visible warning windows.

The Tech Solution

Precision Engineered for Life Safety

A robust IoT architecture designed for 100% reliability even in the harshest local environments.

Non-Contact Detection

Three XKC-Y26-V capacitive sensors detect water through casing, preventing debris interference and ensuring long-term hardware durability.

Hybrid Edge Processing

Arduino Uno handles local hardware interrupts and physical alerts, while the ESP32 manages high-speed wireless cloud synchronization.

Fail-Safe Physical Alerts

An integrated LED tower and 90dB piezo buzzer provide immediate, life-saving local warnings regardless of internet availability.

Level 1: Ready
Level 2: Rising

PWA Synchronization

Cloud-sync via Firebase Realtime Database enables instant remote monitoring through a mobile-first Progressive Web Application.

7.4V Battery System

Dual 18650 Lithium cells

Supplies stable power to microcontrollers, sensors, and alert components, ensuring continuous operation even during power interruptions.

Precision Sensors

3x XKC-Y26-V Non-contact

Detects rising water levels through casing without touching the liquid, identifying flood levels accurately while reducing damage risk.

Dual-Band Sync

ESP32 WiFi + Firebase

Processes sensor data locally and transmits real-time status wirelessly to the PWA via Firebase, enabling sub-second cloud updates.

Hardware Interface

LED Tower + Piezo Buzzer

Provides immediate visual and audible warnings within the community, ensuring safety awareness even without internet or mobile access.

Community Impact

Specifically optimized for the unique topography of Dumingag, Zamboanga del Sur. By combining visual, audible, and digital alerts, we ensure that every resident—regardless of smartphone access—is protected and informed before disaster strikes.
